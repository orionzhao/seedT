5-25 seeds per pack. One of the most unique tomatoes you will ever see, Chile Verde bears plump fruits shaped just like a pepper. The fruits ripen to green, with hints of gold blush. Paste type tomato, with a tart and tangy flavor. A strange look and excellent quality fruits make it an exciting variety. Indeterminate.
